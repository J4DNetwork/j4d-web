Problems We've Heard From Teachers

1

“I spent years getting my handwritten tests into Word”

Teachers invest enormous time digitizing and formatting materials. The content isn't the hard part—the layout, equations, and formatting are.

2

“AI-generated tests don't match what I actually taught”

When teachers try to use AI for full test generation, the results miss curriculum specifics, contain factual errors, or include questions the teacher can't vouch for.

3

“It silently dropped 5 questions and I didn't notice”

AI tools fail in subtle ways. Teachers need workflows where they stay in control and can trust the output—not black-box generators.

4

“I need to know every answer on my own test”

Teacher credibility depends on owning every question. The right AI workflow augments teacher expertise; it doesn't replace it.
